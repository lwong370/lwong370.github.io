Penguin Waddle

Penguin Waddle is a fun spin-off to a running game. Help the penguin jump, roll, and avoid the storms. But beware, the obstacles move faster with time! The penguin is also getting tired, so the jumps are lower and roll time decreases as the game progresses.

Steam Camp 21

This website serves as a resource for thoses interested in incorporating STEAM (Science Technology Engineering Art Math) ideas into their programs and activites. The site was originally built for a community STEAM camp program that hosted week-long STEAM camps for elementary and middle school-aged students.
