Project Purpose

My robotics and girl scout friend needed a website to archive activity manuals for her STEAM camp program. With an interest to develop skills in web development, I hopped on the opportunity to create a simple website for her. With many hours on Google and Youtube tutorials, I was able to make a website for her project.
